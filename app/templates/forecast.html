Machine Learning Model


The dataframe extracted from jupyter notebook contains average stray yield per shire, soil pH and mm of rain. The correlation between the three datasets have been generated. The results give a negative correlation between straw yield and rain fall and between straw yield and soil pH. This makes sense with the rainfall as more rain makes soils more acidic reducing the ability for cereals to grow. However, the pH seems to not have a strong positive correlation with rain. We are fitting the data with a multi-linear regression model where the X are rain and soil and the Y is the straw yield. When we quantify the regression the R2 score gives low accuracy for this model: 0.33, where 1 is the best accuracy.
